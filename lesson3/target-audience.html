The target audience is our community members. We are a resource to business owners and to commnunity members alike.

Persona 1 -- 4th generation store owner

I am the fourth generation of owners in our family business. I grew up in this community as did my parents and my grandparents. This community has supported our family and I want to continue to provide the service and products that they expect. I have recently graduated with a business degree and I am eager to put into action what I have learned. I am also looking for ways to give back to the community that I love. I need the chamber to create networks that provide opportunites to others.

Persona 2 -- New business owner

I have always dreamed of owning my own store and to promote products that I love. I'm not sure how to build community support or how to find the staff I need to run my store. There isn't any competition for my business, so I know that the community will be eager to find my products locally. I would like to be able to hear from other members of the chamber and find out what methods worked or didn't work for them as they started their business.


  1. Why should I join the chamber of commerce?
  2. What resources are available for new businesses?
  3. Are there community events that are sponsored by businesses?
  4. What percentage of the local businesses belong to the chamber?
  5. Would members of the chamber help me learn how to start my own business?
